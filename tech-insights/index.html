How important is CSS in the modern web-dev world?

Obviously the look & feel of your web-site is important. I would even suggest that it's equally important as the information you are trying to display. Every website is trying to give some sort of info to the outside world and you might have the most important bit of information on your site but if it's an ugly or hard to use website. Your message will be lost, forgotten and painfully never seen. In the eternal battle of sizzle vs. steak. You need both.
